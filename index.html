Features

  • Subscription management and automatic feed updates
  • Keeping track of the status of each episode
  • Online search for new feeds to subscribe to
  • Flexible theming and fully configurable keybindings
  • Integration with external programs through MPRIS
  • Mouse support in the terminal-based UI
  • Free and open source license
